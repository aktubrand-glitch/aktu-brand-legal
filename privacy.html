Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

1. Data Collection

AKTU BRAND collects only the essential data needed to provide a secure and personalized experience:

  • User Profile: Name, Email Address, and Gender (for UI personalization).

  • Notification Tokens: FCM tokens are stored to send instant academic alerts.

2. Use of Information

We use your data strictly for:

  • Providing instant notifications regarding AKTU results.

3. Security Infrastructure

We implement professional security measures to protect your account. The application uses global screen-capture prevention to secure your academic results while viewing. All API communications are encrypted via SSL/TLS.

4. Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We use verified services like Google Firebase for notifications and authentication to ensure industry-standard security.
